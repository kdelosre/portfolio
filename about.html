About Me

Welcome and thank you for visiting the portfolio website of Kirstie Delos Reyes! I am currently attending the University of California, Irvine working on a BS in Informatics with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction. I will graduate at the end of the Winter 2017 quarter. I aspire to create or build software or something neat with a bunch of people! I'm a person who also loves fun and being engaged to do and organize activities with others. Bring it on to let me make something cool!

My hobbies include drawing, lion dancing, hanging out with friends, and playing video games. On the side of what I mainly do from the tech industry is artwork. I am also a self-taught artist who picked up drawing as a hobby for 10 years. Much of what I love to do is coming up with character/concept designs and illustrations.
